# Architecture Decision Record Template

## Metadata
- **Status**: Proposed / Accepted / Deprecated / Superseded
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Author**: @username
- **Related Issue**: #123
- **Superseded By**: ADR-XXX (if applicable)

## Context
What is the issue that we're seeing that is motivating this decision or change?

## Decision
What is the change that we're proposing and/or doing?

## Consequences
- **Positive**: What will be easier or better because of this change?
- **Negative**: What will be harder or worse because of this change?
- **Risks**: What are the risks of this decision? How will we mitigate them?

## Alternatives Considered
List alternative approaches that were considered and why they were not chosen.

## Implementation
- **Status**: Not Started / In Progress / Completed
- **Owner**: @username
- **Timeline**: Expected completion date
- **Migration Steps**: (if applicable)
  1. Step 1
  2. Step 2
  3. ...

## References
Links to relevant docs, discussions, or specifications.
